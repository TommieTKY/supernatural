Blackwoods Mansion

   

is a cooperative, audio-based mystery game. Players work together to uncover a haunting story by listening to sound clues linked to cards and arranging the events in the correct order

Setup

  1. Lay Out the Cards: The deck includes 3 chapter cards and 9 fragment cards, each with a unique illustration. Spread them out so everyone can see

  2. Scan the Cards: Use your phone's camera to scan a card's QR code and listen to its audio clue, which hints at an event in the story.

  3. Discuss and Connect: Talk with your teammates about how the sounds and images might be linked.

  4. Build Chapters: The game has 3 chapters. Each chapter includes 3 related fragment cards. Work together to group the correct fragments and place them in the right order for each chapter.

Winning the Game

  • Go to the “Solution” page to test your theory.

  • Select 3 fragment cards and arrange them in the order you believe they occurred within a chapter.

  • The website will confirm if your sequence is correct.

Once all 3 chapters are correctly solved, the full story will be revealed through a final narrated audio.
